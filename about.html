About Us

Welcome to Red Falcon Cannabis Dispensary located in Coachella, California!

Red Falcon proudly stands as a beacon of community. Born from the esteemed legacy of the Twenty-Nine Palms Band of Mission Indians, Red Falcon blends centuries-old traditions with modern cannabis offerings to redefine the local landscape.

Rooted in the sacred stories of our ancestors, the Red Falcon symbolizes courage, strength, and wisdom. We pay homage to this revered guardian with every plant cultivated and product curated, ensuring that our offerings reflect the essence of our heritage
