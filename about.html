About Us

Welcome to Red Falcon Cannabis Dispensary located in Coachella, California!

Red Falcon proudly stands as a beacon of community. Born from the esteemed legacy of the Twenty-Nine Palms Band of Mission Indians, Red Falcon blends centuries-old traditions with modern cannabis offerings to redefine the local landscape.

Rooted in the sacred stories of our ancestors, the Red Falcon symbolizes courage, strength, and wisdom. We pay homage to this revered guardian with every plant cultivated and product curated, ensuring that our offerings reflect the essence of our heritage

Empowering Community

More than just a cannabis dispensary, Red Falcon is a gathering place — a space where neighbors become friends and new faces are welcomed. We believe in the power of cannabis to unite and uplift, and we strive to foster a culture of inclusivity and respect in everything we do.

Elevating Experiences

From premium cannabis flower to artisanal edibles and beyond, our carefully curated selection caters to every taste and preference. Our friendly staff is here to offer expert advice, guidance, and personalized recommendations.

Empowering Our People

As a tribal enterprise, Red Falcon is committed to creating economic opportunities and fostering sustainable growth within our community. By investing in local talent, supporting small businesses, and giving back to causes that matter, we aim to build a brighter future for generations to come.
